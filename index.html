04 · Strategy

THE PLAN.
THREE STEPS.
ONE DIRECTION.

Everything runs in sequence. Build the right foundation, then activate the channels, then scale. No shortcuts — and no ad spend until step one is complete.

01

Rebuild the website

Real photography. Native booking flow. Brand voice. Dedicated vehicle pages. Social proof. The digital storefront rebuilt to match the standard of the service.

02

Launch Google Search

Target high-intent keywords — luxury chauffeur, airport transfers, specialty fleet, Long Island and Hamptons. Intercept buyers at the exact moment of decision.

03

Activate Meta & TikTok

Fleet visuals, paid social, and monthly content for the occasion buyer — date nights, events, anniversaries, NYC lifestyle. Build brand and convert simultaneously.
